Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist

Do’s:
- Think before posting and ensure your content is appropriate
- Maintain a positive and professional online presence
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
- Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely
- Use privacy settings to protect your personal information

Don’ts:
- Do not share sensitive personal details (address, phone number, etc.)
- Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown sources
- Do not overshare content that could harm your reputation
