## Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)

### Do’s:
Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
Verify links and emails before clicking, especially those asking for login or payment details
Keep your apps and device software updated to avoid security vulnerabilities
Download apps only from trusted sources like official app stores
Regularly review privacy settings on social media accounts

### UPI / Financial Safety:
Never approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps; receiving money does not require approval
Do not share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank
Double-check QR codes before scanning, especially in public places or online


### Don’ts:
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions
Do not share personal details like address, ID proofs, or phone number publicly
Avoid clicking on suspicious links sent via SMS, email, or messaging apps


### Report Cybercrime:
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline: 1930
